Account Deletion Request

RV Archive App

⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to permanently delete your account and all associated data before proceeding.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your RV Archive account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team at: cjpienta@proton.me
  2. Include the following information in your email:
    • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
    • Your registered email address
    • Your username (if different from email)
    • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
  3. We will process your request within 30 days
  4. You will receive confirmation once your account has been deleted

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your user account information (email, username, password)
  • All remote viewing session data and submissions
  • Any uploaded images or files
  • Your profile information and preferences
  • Any favorites or bookmarks you've created
  • All personal data associated with your account

Data Retention

After your deletion request:

  • Personal data: Deleted within 30 days
  • Backup systems: Data may remain in backups for up to 90 days for technical reasons
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Alternative Options

If you don't want to delete your entire account, you may also:

  • Delete individual remote viewing sessions from within the app
  • Update your profile information
  • Change your privacy settings
  • Contact us about specific data concerns

Contact Information

Email: cjpienta@proton.me

Subject Line: Account Deletion Request

Response Time: We aim to respond within 7 business days

Processing Time: Account deletion completed within 30 days
